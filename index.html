Memory Gift Atelier
Memory Gift Atelier is a digital gifting ritual.

Each Sanrio character carries an emotional frequency — a quiet mood, a burst of energy, a soft comfort. By selecting and assembling them into a gift box, you're not just collecting cute things. You're curating a feeling.

The gift you build becomes a small constellation of who you are right now.
How to play
Drag a character from the bottom dock onto the Exhibition Stage to learn about them
Drag characters into the gift box on the right to collect them (up to 5)
Once you've gathered your selection, click the box to reveal your gift message
Or hit ✦ Surprise me! to let the Atelier choose for you
